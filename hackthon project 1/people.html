If the member you’re searching for is your connection, their profile(s) is displayed at the top of the search results page. You can either message them or view their profile from the search results page. You might also see different sections displaying the member’s activity and posts mentioning the member.
If the member you’re searching for is not your connection, then depending on the member’s profile settings, you can view their profile, Follow, or Connect with them from the search results page. From the People section, you can click on See all people results to view more members sharing the same first or last name. From any search results page, click the People tab at the top to only see people results for your search. You can further sort and filter your results by clicking the dropdown next to each category
